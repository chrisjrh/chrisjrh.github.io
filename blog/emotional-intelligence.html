What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is also referred to as EQ. It is the capacity of understanding feelings and being able to deal with those feelings calmly. It is also the ability to control and evaluate emotions from yourself and other around you.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ and EQ are very different and the differences between the two are, IQ is about the ability to solve problems using logic or grasp complex idea. Whereas EQ is about your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others.

Why is it Important?

EQ is important becuase it can help with relationships and can also help people work better together in teams. If you can understand someone elses feelings you can understand when you are offeneding someone and when to stop.
