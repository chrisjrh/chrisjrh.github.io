This means modable brain and it states that the brain can change throughout life, it means if you are exposed to something enough you will learn it from the ability of your brain to modify and re-wire itself. Growth Mindset

Growth Mindset is all about your mental attitude and making you believe you have the ability to improve. Its about not giving up if you fail but instead learning from failure and doing better the next time. I've always believed I have had a pretty good mental attitude so when I fail at something ofcourse its still frustrating however I like to believe I am able to learn from those mistakes.
